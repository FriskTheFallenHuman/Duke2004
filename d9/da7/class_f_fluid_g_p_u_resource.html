Parameters
DeltaTime- amount of time passed since last update
FluidForces- array of forces that should be applied
DetailUpdateRate- frequency of simulation steps
DetailDamping- amount to uniformly dampen the simulation
DetailTravelSpeed- wave travel speed factor
DetailTransfer- amount of each force to apply
DetailHeightScale- scale on the height before calculating normals
