Enumeration of all allowed output formats These flags are used in OutputOptions::mAllowedFormats.

Enumerator

Format_L8 

Luminance 8 bits.

Format_RGBA16 

Color RGBA 16 bits per components (64bpp)

Format_L16 

Luminance 16 bits.

Format_BC2

Format_Mask_Raw16bpc 

L16,RGBA16.

Format_Mask_Compressed

Format_Mask_ALL
