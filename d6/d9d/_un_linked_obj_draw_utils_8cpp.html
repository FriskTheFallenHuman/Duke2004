Adjusts connectors after they have been moved so that they do not overlap. This is done by sorting the connectors based on their (possibly) overlapping positions. Once they are sorted, we recompute their positions based on their sorted order so that they are spaced evenly and do not overlap but still maintain relative position based on their sorted order.

Parameters
PlacementDataAn array of connector placement data to be adjusted
PosThe position of the sequence object where these connectors reside
SizeThe size of the sequence object where these connectors reside.
VarSpaceThe amount of space we have to place connectors
AdjustYWhether or not we are adjusting in the Y direction (since spacing is computed differently).
