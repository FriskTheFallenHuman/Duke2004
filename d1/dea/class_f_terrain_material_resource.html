◆ PostLoad()

void FTerrainMaterialResource::PostLoad ( )

Called after deserialization of the material during loading.

Called after the terrain material resource has been loaded to verify the underlying material GUIDs that were stored with the resource. If any are different, the shader is tossed and has to be recompiled.
