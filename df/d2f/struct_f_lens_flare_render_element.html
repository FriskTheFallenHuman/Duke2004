◆ RayDistance

FLOAT FLensFlareRenderElement::RayDistance

The position along the ray from the source to the viewpoint to render the flare at. 0.0 = At the source 1.0 = The source point reflected about the view center. < 0 = The point along the ray going away from the center past the source.

‍1 = The point along the ray beyond the 'end point' of the ray reflection.
