Utlity for turning arbitary mesh into convex hulls. @output OutGeom Collection of convex that will be filled in by function. OutGeom.ConvexElems will be emptied and re-filled by this utility.

Parameters
InVerticesArray of vertex positions of input mesh
InIndicesArray of triangle indices for input mesh
InDepthDepth to split, a maximum of 10, generally not over 7
InConcavityThreshThreshold amount of convexity for splitting, between 0.0 and 1.0 (0.0-0.2 is reasonable)
InCollapseThreshThreshold volume preservation to collapse, between 0.0 and 1.0 (0.0-0.3 is reasonable)
InMaxHullVertsNumber of verts allowed in a hull
